What are Solar Panels

Solar panels are devices that convert light into electricity. They are called "solar" panels because most of the time, the most powerful source of light available is the Sun.

Why Do We Need Solar Energy?

Unlike fossil fuels, solar energy doesn't produce the harmful pollutants. Solar energy is a clean and sustainable source of energy that you can use. Source
